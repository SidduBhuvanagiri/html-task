The Red Line Metro Hyderabad operates daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM in 2024. Trains run at 5-minute intervals during peak hours (8:00-11:00 AM and 5:00-8:00 PM) and 10-minute intervals during off-peak times. The last train departs from terminal stations at 10:15 PM. Service frequency may vary on weekends and holidays.
